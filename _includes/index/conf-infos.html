The 8th edition of Incontro DevOps Italia (IDI2020) will take place ONLINE on Wednesday 21st and Thursday 22ns October 2020. We are looking forward to meet you again this year!

Incontro DevOps is a conference about DevOps, Continuous Delivery, Cloud, Microservices, Automation, Testing, Big Data, Containers, Data Center Operating Systems, Software Operability, IT operating models and how to plan and organise IT Operations.

The day after Incontro DevOps, another event is happening online: on Friday, October 23rd 2020 you can attend containerday - the italian conference dedicated to container and virtualisation technologies.

Why join?

Whether you're a novice or an expert, grab the opportunity to learn something new from people using those technologies and methodologies every day.
