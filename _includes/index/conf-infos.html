The 8th edition of Incontro DevOps Italia (IDI2020) will take place in Bologna on Thursday, October 22nd 2020. We are looking forward to meet you again this year!

Incontro DevOps is a conference about DevOps, Continuous Delivery, Cloud, Microservices, Automation, Testing, Big Data, Containers, Data Center Operating Systems, Software Operability, IT operating models and how to plan and organise IT Operations.

During the event, we will also host lightning talks and there will be an open space available. The day before the conference (21st October 2020) our partners will present few workshops. more news will appear on Facebook and on Twitter (@incontrodevops).

The day after the conference (Friday, October 23rd 2020) containerday will take place in the same venue. containerday is the italian conference dedicated to container and virtualisation technologies.

Why join?

Whether you're a novice or an expert, grab the opportunity to learn something new from people using those technologies and methodologies every day.
